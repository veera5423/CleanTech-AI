Where Can CleanTech Be Used?

CleanTech provides smart waste classification solutions that can be applied across multiple domains, from cities to schools, helping build a sustainable future.

Municipal Waste

Smart bins powered by AI can classify and segregate waste instantly, reducing manual effort and human error.

Recycling Plants

Automated classification improves recycling efficiency and minimizes contamination in recyclable materials.

Educational Awareness

Schools and universities can integrate CleanTech into awareness campaigns to teach sustainable waste practices.

Smart Cities

Integrated with IoT, CleanTech enables smart bins, data-driven decisions, and real-time waste monitoring.
