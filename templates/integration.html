How CleanTech Can Be Integrated

CleanTech is designed to fit seamlessly into existing waste management infrastructures, providing flexible and scalable solutions for modern cities.

IoT Smart Bins

Deploy CleanTech-powered smart bins that classify waste at the point of disposal with instant AI predictions.

Waste Collection Trucks

Equip garbage trucks with onboard CleanTech AI systems to sort and classify waste in real-time during collection.

Industrial Sorting Units

Large-scale recycling facilities can use CleanTech to increase efficiency and reduce contamination in waste streams.

Mobile Applications

Engage citizens through apps that allow users to scan and classify waste items, promoting awareness and participation.
